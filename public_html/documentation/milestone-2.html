Content Strategy

Welcome:

There will be an introduction of my name and 3-4 keywords to describe who I am on the left side. The text will be overlaid on a large photo.

About Me:

My about me will be a short and sweet overview of my background. While adding details about my passion for all things design and web development. I want to give glimpses into who I am outside of work, like a dog lover with two creative businesses. I'd like to add icons that pertain to my hobbies and likes.I want to set a fun and personable tone with this section because it fits my personality and overall environment I would love to work in. I will add another photo of myself to the right of the text.

Portfolio:

I would like for this to be more of a resume section where I include my education, certifications, and experience with content creation. As a new web developer, my experience is limited but I would like to add our capstone project with a photo and link inviting users to check it out! There will be sections for my work exerience, projects, etc. and anytime you hover over the text, a text bubble with more details will pop up and disappear when you move your mouse away. For the mobile site, you will have to click on an arrow to the right of the sections and the details for each section will appear.

Contact:

This section will have a contact form with a witty or sweet, "get in touch" with me message. The footer will include links to my github, linkedin, twitter, and instagram page, and possibly my professional address.
